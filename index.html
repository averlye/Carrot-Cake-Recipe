Carrot Cake

A piece of heaven.

PREP 20 min

COOK 45 min

TOTAL 1 hr 5 min


Ingredients

For carrot cake










For the frosting




Preparation

Heat the oven to 180 Celcius degrees.
Grease and flour the bottom and sides of a 22cm cake pan.

Batter

In a medium bowl mix: flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon until well-blended.

In a separate bowl mix: oil, sugar and vanilla. Whisk in eggs, one at a time, until combined.

Scrape sides and bottom of the bowl with wet ingredients.

Add dry ingredients in 3 parts, gently stirring until they disappear and your batter is smooth.

Stir in grated carrots, nuts or raisins.

Moving on

Put batter into the greased and flouered pan.

Bake until the top of the cake is springy when touched and when a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean - 35 to 45 minutes.

Cool the cake in a pan for 15 minutes after baking, then turn around, take out of the form and cool the other side.

Last steps

In a large bowl beat cream cheese with a handheld mixer on medium speed until creamy - about 1 minute.

Beat in the powdered sugar, a 1/4 cup at a time until fluffy.

Chill covered until ready to frost cake.

When the cake is completely cool cut in half to create two layers.

Frost the top of one cake layer, place the other cake layer on top.
